GroupMe Bot

This was a fun side project of mine. I had a friend who kept getting kicked from our Fraternity GroupMe, and would subsuquentaly be out of the GroupMe more than he was in it.

How and What does it do?

Simply put, it scans the GroupMe for someone getting removed from it, it then quickly cross checks the user ID that was removed to see if it was this particular friend, or myself and re-adds them. Runs or Heroku for free.
