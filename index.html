Latest Projects

SocialPnt - IOS App

A location-based social network that connects people through places.

Users can engage with their friends’ past, present, and future through the use of distinct Pnts located in popular locations. Now available on the App store for free!

Framework, services and more

SocialPnt leverages the Ionic framework to give a cross-platform mobile experience. It utilizes Google Firebase, and their Cloud Firestore service for it's database purposes. Some other API's that it utilizes are Algolia Search, Branch.io, and Cloudinary Image hosting

Languages

Socialpnt is written in Typescript, HTML and SCSS with a touch of Swift and Objective-C where needed.

LastCam - An Ionic-Cordova Camera Plugin written in Swift

LastCam is a plugin I created after finding some of the plugins within the Ionic community to be lacking. It utilizes a users camera to create a camera preview that is streamed straight from their phone, into any HTML view that they want.

The thing that makes LastCam special is it's ability to capture both videos, and images, while other plugins only offer one or the other.

Languages

LastCam is written in Swift, Javascript and Objective-C

YTrap - IOS App

YTrap is a simple mobile app review system designed to allow the attendees of an event to give a honest yet anonymous review of the event without outing themselves to facilitate better events in the future.

YTrap was the first mobile app that I made. After careful consideration I made the decision to shut the app down, after some privacy concerns with the Facebook Developer API, which fueled the application.

Freedom Summer App - IOS & Android App

The Freedom Summer App (FSApp) is a location-based tour that uses a mobile device and GPS technology to transport you to 1964 to train as a Freedom Summer volunteer on Miami University's campus in Oxford, Ohio.

Languages

FSApp is build using the open-source project ARIS, written in PHP and Objective-C. It leverages augmented reality to immerse the user into the rich history of the Freedom Summer, while using real-time image recognition to project historical images onto the users screen at certain locations. The app is currently in Beta, and only available to pre-approved testers.

Other Projects

GroupMe Bot Private

This was a fun side project of mine. I had a friend who kept getting kicked from our Fraternity GroupMe, and would subsequently be out of the GroupMe more than he was in it.

Simply put, the bot watches the GroupMe and waits for a notification that someone was removed. Once the notification is found, it quickly cross-checks the user ID that was removed to see if it was this particular friend, or myself and re-adds the user.

Languages

The GroupMe bot is written in Python and is hosted on Heroku for free.

Ionic Long-standing issue documentation

This was an issue that I originally opened with the Ionic team, but unfortunately was left unanswered, despite receiving quite some notoriety within the community. The issue was with their production build command running out of memory, even on high-end devices due to memory-leakages with some of their dependencies and the recursive use of file-paths.

After three months of testing out fixes, I came up with a solution, that solved the issue at large.
